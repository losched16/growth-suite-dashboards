# Welcome to Growth Suite — Your Marketing Launch, Step by Step

*(Client-facing — send as PDF or paste into the welcome email)*

Welcome! We're excited to help more families discover your school. This page
explains exactly what happens over your first two weeks, what we handle, and
the handful of things we'll need from you. Keep it handy.

## The two-week launch plan

**Week 1 — Setup & Strategy**
- **Days 1–3 · Tech setup (us, with a little help from you).** We build your
  marketing system: your CRM workspace, tour-booking calendar, a dedicated
  web address for your campaign pages, a professional sending address for
  email, and a local phone number for inquiries.
- **Days 3–7 · Your messaging.** You'll complete a short **Messaging Intake
  Form** (about 20 minutes — please finish it before your call), then join a
  **60-minute messaging call with Tim Seldin**, one of the most respected
  voices in Montessori education. This call shapes everything we write for
  you.

**Week 2 — Build & Launch**
- **Days 7–10 · We write and design.** Using your intake form and the Tim
  call, we create your ad copy, your tour landing page, and your automated
  email sequences — all in your school's voice.
- **Days 10–14 · We build and launch.** Your "Schedule a Tour" campaign goes
  live: ads, landing page, automated confirmations and reminders, plus your
  **"Is Montessori Right For Me?" quiz** — a friendly way for curious parents
  to raise their hands early.

## What you get

- A **"Schedule a Tour" ad campaign** (Facebook + Instagram) with
  professionally written copy and custom images built from *your real
  school photos*
- A **tour landing page** on your own web address where parents book
  directly into your calendar
- **Automatic emails and texts**: instant booking confirmations, reminders
  (fewer no-shows!), post-tour follow-ups, and a nurture series for parents
  who aren't ready yet
- The **"Is Montessori Right For Me?" quiz** lead magnet
- A **weekly parent newsletter**, professionally templated, featuring one
  quality Montessori article every week from our content library — you just
  send us your announcements
- Every inquiry organized in one place, automatically tagged and tracked
  from first click to booked tour

## What we need from you (this is the whole list)

**In the first 3 days:**
1. ✅ Access: Facebook Page + Instagram, and your Facebook Ad Account (we'll
   guide you — about 15 minutes on a screen-share). Ad spend bills directly
   to your card with Meta; we never touch it.
2. ✅ A quick DNS change by whoever manages your website (we send exact
   copy-paste instructions — takes them ~5 minutes)
3. ✅ Your tour availability (which days/times you give tours) + a calendar
   connection for the person who gives them
4. ✅ Your EIN (required by phone carriers to register your texting number —
   a US regulation for all businesses)
5. ✅ **15–20 real photos** of your school: classrooms in action, outdoor
   spaces, teachers, your building. Real photos dramatically outperform
   stock photography. (Only send photos of children whose families have
   signed media releases.)

**In week 1:**
6. ✅ Complete the Messaging Intake Form *before* your Tim Seldin call
7. ✅ Attend the 60-minute messaging call (decision-makers please!)

**Ongoing (very light):**
8. ✅ A quick weekly note with any school announcements for your newsletter
9. ✅ **Answer new inquiries fast.** Speed matters more than anything else we
   do — a tour inquiry answered within an hour books at several times the
   rate of one answered the next day. Our automations respond instantly, but
   when a parent calls or replies personally, that's your moment.

## What to expect from results

Ads typically take **1–2 weeks of learning** before lead flow stabilizes —
early fluctuation is normal and expected. We watch performance continuously
and adjust copy, images, and targeting. You'll hear from us with a summary
of leads, tours booked, and what we're optimizing.
