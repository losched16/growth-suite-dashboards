Portal Forms Knowledge Base

Everything your school needs to know about parent-portal forms.

Portal Forms are forms your families fill out inside their authenticated parent portal. They’re different from public lead-capture forms because they already know who the parent is, which children belong to them, and what data is on file — so parents only fill out what we don’t already know.

This knowledge base walks through everything from the basics (what they are and why to use them) to the practical details (per-student vs per-family, signatures, Growth Suite writeback) to advanced settings.
