# Family Portal — FAQ & Walkthrough

**For Montessori School of Wooster families**

Welcome to the Family Portal. This is your one-stop spot for school
paperwork, your family's contact info, and the forms we need from you
each year. This guide walks through everything inside.

> The portal is also available inside the app — sign in and click
> **Help** in the left nav (or **Help** in the mobile nav) for a
> condensed, interactive version of this same guide.

---

## Part 1 — Signing in

### First time

1. We sent you an email titled *"Your Wooster Family Portal is ready"*.
   Open it and click the sign-in link.
2. The link takes you to a page asking for the email this message was
   sent to. Type it in.
3. We email you a **one-time sign-in link**. Click it (within 15
   minutes — links expire).
4. Set a password. You're in.

### Coming back

- **Same device:** the portal keeps you signed in for a few weeks.
  You can just open the link.
- **New device or signed out:** go to <https://family.woomontessori.org/login>,
  enter your email, click the link we send you, and you're back.
- **Forgot your password?** Same flow — enter your email, we email a
  link, click it, set a new password.

### Why don't we use a username?

Your email IS your username. It's also where the school office sends
notifications and where your sign-in link arrives, so keeping them in
sync means one less thing to remember.

## Part 2 — Your Home page

The first page you see when you sign in. It tells you, at a glance:

- **How many kids you have at the school** and what classroom they're in
- **Forms still needed for the year** — anything red or yellow needs
  your attention; anything green is done
- **Parents on file** — both you and your co-parent (if added)
- **Shortcuts** to the most-used sections (Family, Forms)

If your Home page is all green checks, you're set. The portal is then
just a place to look up info or make changes during the year.

## Part 3 — Family

Click **Family** in the left nav.

### Your contact info

Edit your first name, last name, and phone. Your email is what you
sign in with — to change it, email the school office.

### Your co-parent

If a second parent is already listed, you'll see them as a read-only
card. They can edit their own info when they sign in.

If a second parent is **not** listed yet, scroll down to the
**"Add another parent"** section (it's collapsed — click to expand).

- Only first and last name are required.
- Email is optional. Add it if you want them to have their own portal
  sign-in. Check the "Email them a sign-in link" box and we'll send
  them a welcome message.

### Per-child assignments (blended families)

If you have a blended family — for example, you're the biological
parent to kid A and a step-parent to kid B — you can scope yourself
to specific kids using the **"I'm a parent of"** picker on your card.
Default is every kid in the family.

### Privacy toggle

For divorced or separated families. The **"Keep my contact info
private from the other parent(s)"** checkbox masks your email and
phone from the other parent's portal view. The school office still
sees your full record.

### Students

Each kid has their own card. Editable fields:

- Allergies (and notes / severity)
- Health care provider name + phone

Read-only school-managed fields (DOB, classroom, teacher, IEP/504,
schedule) appear at the top of the card. To change any of those,
email the school office.

### Emergency contacts (up to 5)

Below the Students grid is the **Emergency Contacts** card. Click
**Add contacts** (or **Edit / add more**) to open the Emergency
Medical form.

- Up to 5 structured contacts (name + phone + relationship)
- A free-form section for any extras beyond 5
- Each contact can be assigned to specific kids or "all students"

### Authorized pickup people

Separate list from emergency contacts. Use this for grandparents,
nannies, family friends — anyone the school is allowed to release
your kids to.

Click **Manage who can pick up your students** → add a person with
name, phone, relationship, and (for multi-kid families) which kids
they're authorized to pick up.

## Part 4 — Forms

Click **Forms** in the left nav.

### Reading the page

- A **green check** means the form is done for the year.
- **Yellow** means partially done (some kids done, some not — for
  forms that are per-student).
- **Red** means the form is required and not yet started.

### Filling out a form

- Most fields pre-fill from what you submitted in previous years.
- For per-student forms, you'll see one section per kid. Each kid's
  info is independent — you can save Allergies for kid A and come
  back later to do kid B.
- **Saving:** click **Save**. Your progress is kept; you can come
  back later without losing what you typed.
- **Submitting:** sign at the bottom and click **Lock & submit**.
  The school office is notified by email immediately.

### Required forms for the 2026 school year

- Emergency Medical
- Enrollment Agreement
- Health History
- Health Conditions
- Injury History
- Medications
- Media Permission
- ODE Connectivity
- (Plus any event-specific forms — currently the class trip preview)

### Printing or saving a copy

After you submit a form, the school can email you the receipt — or
you can print a copy from the portal:

1. Open any submitted form from the Forms page
2. Click **Print / Save as PDF** at the top
3. In the print dialog, change Destination to **Save as PDF**

### Editing after you submit

The portal lets you edit and resubmit anytime. The school office is
notified of every new submission, so they always see the latest.

## Part 5 — Common scenarios

### "My family didn't get the welcome email"

Check spam first. If it's not there:

- Confirm the email you expect it at is the one the school has on
  file. Email the office to check.
- Ask the office to resend the welcome workflow from their dashboard.
- As a workaround, anyone with an email on file can sign in directly
  at <https://family.woomontessori.org/login> — typing their email
  triggers a fresh sign-in link.

### "I see a duplicate student or a misspelled name"

Email the office. The school staff has tools to merge duplicates and
correct names. Don't try to "delete" a student from the portal —
there isn't a way to (intentionally — student records are managed
school-side to prevent accidental deletes).

### "My child's last name is different from mine"

That's fine and supported. When you (or the school) updates a parent's
last name in our system, only students who currently share the
parent's old last name are automatically renamed. Students with a
different last name are left alone — so step-children and kids with
different surnames in blended families are safe.

### "I want to update both parents' emergency-contact list to be different"

By default, emergency contacts on the Emergency Medical form are
**family-level** — both parents see the same list, both parents'
edits write to the same list. If you actually need different
emergency contacts for different parents (rare), email the office —
they can configure that on a case-by-case basis.

### "How does the school see my updates? When?"

Instantly. Every form submission and every family-info edit appears
in the school's dashboard within seconds. The office also gets email
notifications on every form submission.

### "Can I delete my account?"

You can stop using the portal at any time — no further action needed.
We don't delete contact records from your school's CRM since they're
part of your students' enrollment record. If you're moving away or
withdrawing, just let the office know and they'll mark you accordingly.

## Part 6 — Privacy & security

- Your information is visible only to Wooster staff and the parents
  on your family record.
- Sign-in links from your email expire after **15 minutes** and can
  only be used **once**.
- You're signed out automatically after a long period of inactivity.
  Sign back in any time.
- The portal uses HTTPS for every page. Your password is hashed
  (not stored in plain text).
- If you ever suspect someone got access they shouldn't have, email
  the office to reset.
