What is the Parent Portal?

Article 1 of 12 · Parent Portal Knowledge Base

The Parent Portal is your school’s branded, family-facing website inside Growth Suite. Every enrolled family logs into the same place to check their student’s attendance, fill out forms, manage authorized pickup people, see their tuition, upload documents, and message the school. It replaces a dozen scattered tools (paper sign-in sheets, Google Forms, email threads, spreadsheets) with one consistent experience for families and one source of truth for your office.

Who it’s for

The Portal is built for the people in your families — mom, dad, grandparent, guardian, anyone with a parental role at your school. Each parent has their own login but sees the same shared family data. Children don’t log in directly.

What it does

Section of the portalWhat parents do there
HomeSee today’s overview — who’s checked in, what’s pending, what messages need a response.
AttendanceCheck students in for the day, check them out at pickup, see attendance history.
FamilyView and update parent contact info, see student details, manage household information.
FormsFill out school forms (permission slips, medical updates, releases) per student or per family.
DocumentsUpload immunization records, custody papers, insurance cards — anything the school office asks for.
MessagesSee and reply to messages from the school, with the full thread preserved.
TuitionSee current balance, payment plan, and payment history.
Financial AidApply for, track, and submit documents for tuition assistance.
SettingsManage authorized pickup people and account preferences.

Why it matters for your school

  • Less office-on-fire moments. When parents have self-service for the things they used to call about (check the attendance, update an emergency contact, see the tuition balance), your front desk gets dramatically fewer interruptions.
  • Audit trail by default. Every submission, sign-in, upload, and message is timestamped and stored. When a question comes up (“did Mrs. Brown ever send the immunization form?”), you have an answer in seconds.
  • Data integrity. Information parents update flows directly into their Growth Suite contact record. There’s no “update the spreadsheet, update the database, update the email list” double-entry.
  • Mobile-first by design. Parents fill out forms, sign their kid in, and reply to messages from their phone — in the school parking lot, in the carpool line, in the doctor’s office.

How it’s different from a generic family-engagement app

  • It’s yours, not ours. The portal is branded for your school — your logo, your colors, your support email. Parents don’t see “Growth Suite” or any other vendor name.
  • It’s connected to your operations. Forms, attendance, messages, tuition, and family data all share the same backbone. A change in one place is visible everywhere.
  • It scales with your school. Whether you have 40 students or 400, the experience and the cost stay the same.

What the Portal is not

To set expectations clearly:

  • It’s not a learning management system. It doesn’t host curriculum, assignments, or grades.
  • It’s not a public-facing website — that’s separate. The Portal is for enrolled families.
  • It’s not a replacement for in-person communication with teachers — it complements it.
