Parent Portal Knowledge Base

Everything your school needs to know about the family-facing portal.

The Parent Portal is your school’s branded, family-facing site inside Growth Suite. Every enrolled family logs in to check attendance, fill out forms, manage authorized pickup people, see tuition, upload documents, and message the school — all in one place.

This knowledge base walks through what the portal does, how to brand it, how each section works, and how to roll it out to your families. For deep coverage of the Forms feature specifically, see the separate Portal Forms Knowledge Base.
